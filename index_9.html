About Matt

Why Me?

I'm a 21-year-old photographer based in Morris County, NJ. I started with a camera in my hands and never really put it down - what began as a passion turned into something I get to do for real people, real moments, and real memories.

My style is cinematic and intentional - I love the drama of light, the depth of a great composition, and images that make you feel something when you look at them.

Whether it's your wedding day, a headshot for your next big move, or a property that needs to stand out - I show up like a friend, not just a photographer.

let me help tell your story."
